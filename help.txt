[+] To start sniffing traffic in PySniffer, launch it in a terminal launched from the PySniffer v* folders with the command
- sudo -E python3 PySniffer.py . Then launch the sniffer and select the type of traffic you want to monitor.
After turning it off, launch the sniffer by pressing the start button, and the packet traffic will begin to be displayed in a text table. 
You can save it in .pcap format.
